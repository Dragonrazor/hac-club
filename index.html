Parakeets

Parakeets, also known as budgerigars or budgies, are small, colorful parrots that are popular as pets due to their sociable and playful nature. Originating from Australia, parakeets are known for their bright plumage, which can come in a variety of colors, including green, blue, yellow, and white. They are intelligent birds that can mimic human speech and sounds, making them a delight for bird enthusiasts. Parakeets thrive in social environments and enjoy the company of other birds or human interaction. They require a balanced diet, regular mental stimulation, and a spacious cage to stay healthy and happy. With proper care, these charming birds can live up to 10-15 years, bringing joy and companionship to their owners.
